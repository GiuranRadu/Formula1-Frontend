Formula 1, commonly known as F1, is the pinnacle of motorsport, a globally acclaimed racing spectacle that combines cutting-edge technology, top-tier engineering, and exceptional driver skill. Established in 1950, F1 has evolved into a high-stakes competition featuring a series of Grand Prix events held on iconic tracks around the world.
